On WKWebView detection, render a full-screen centered div with heading "Open in Safari to use Dead Letter Diary" and instruction text "Tap the share button and choose 'Open in Safari' or 'Open in Browser'." Otherwise render `{children}`. Accept `children: React.ReactNode` prop.

    **Create apps/web/components/InstallPrompt.tsx** (`"use client"`):
    Handles two paths:
    1. iOS (UA contains iPhone|iPad|iPod) + not standalone (`!window.matchMedia('(display-mode: standalone)').matches`): Show step-by-step coaching: "To install: tap the Share button (the box with an arrow), then tap 'Add to Home Screen'."     2. Desktop/Android: Listen for `beforeinstallprompt` event. Store the event. Render an "Install app" button that calls `deferredPrompt.prompt()`. On `appinstalled` event, hide the prompt.
    Render null when neither condition applies or user has dismissed.

    **Create apps/web/components/SwUpdateToast.tsx** (`"use client"`):
    Accept props: `hasUnsavedText: boolean`. On mount, call `navigator.serviceWorker.register('/sw.js', { scope: '/', updateViaCache: 'none' })` and listen for `updatefound` / `statechange` / `waiting` to detect a waiting SW. When a waiting SW is detected, show a toast div (fixed position, bottom-right) with text "Update available — restart to apply." and an "Update now" button. The button: if `hasUnsavedText` is true, show an alert "Save or clear your text before updating"; otherwise, call `registration.waiting.postMessage({ type: 'SKIP_WAITING' })` and reload. Also re-check `registration.waiting` on component mount (handles case where page loaded with a waiting SW already).
